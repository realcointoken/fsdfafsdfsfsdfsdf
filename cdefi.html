What is cDeFi?‍
cDEFI is an equally weighted index-style, liquidity provider (LP) token which seeks to capture upside from the most promising projects in Celo DeFi.
‍
What are the weights ?
cDEFI is a neutrally constructed and equally-weighted token. It has a 25% target weight for each of the underlying tokens.

how is it rebalanced?
It has 25% target weights and is positioned to be continually rebalanced by creating arbitrage opportunities for participants on the platform.
‍
What are the risks?
In addition to idiosyncratic risk (project specific risk), cDEFI token holders are also exposed to network specific and smart contract risk. Please make sure you understand these risks before participating.
‍
It is important to note, if any one token in the cDEFI pool goes to zero, the value of the entire cDEFI pool could go to zero. Therefore, it is strongly recommended to only use capital you are comfortable losing in its entirety.
